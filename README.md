# Rover Clone

A simplified clone of the Rover pet services app, focused on two core services.

## Scope (MVP)

### In Scope
- **Boarding**: Pet owners can book stays at a sitter's home
- **Dog Walking**: Pet owners can book walks with local walkers

### Out of Scope (for now)
- House sitting (sitter comes to owner)
- Drop-in visits
- Doggy day care
- Payment processing (mock only)
- Background checks
- Insurance
- Mobile apps (web only)

## Tech Stack (TBD after refinement)
- Frontend: React/Next.js or similar
- Backend: Node.js/Express or similar
- Database: PostgreSQL or similar
- Auth: Simple JWT or session-based
